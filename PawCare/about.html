Who We Are

PawCare Foundation is a non-profit animal welfare organization dedicated to rescuing abandoned, injured, and homeless dogs. Founded with compassion and commitment, we work towards creating a safer and kinder environment for animals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue animals in distress, provide them with proper medical care, and ensure they find loving and responsible homes. We also aim to spread awareness about animal welfare and responsible pet ownership.

Our Vision

We envision a society where no animal is left to suffer on the streets and every pet is treated with dignity, love, and care. Through education and community involvement, we strive to reduce animal cruelty and abandonment.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike many shelters, PawCare Foundation focuses on rehabilitation and emotional healing of rescued dogs. Each animal receives individual attention, medical support, and behavioral care before adoption.
